About Me

Hi there! I'm a software engineer with a strong passion for healthcare.

My academic background is in natural language processsing (NLP), a field I was first exposed to while studying psychology under Dr. Peter Gordon at my alma mater, UNC. Through my NLP interest I've worked with some of the most popular machine learning and data science techniques.

Healthcare has always been a strong interest for me; I considered medical school and graduate studies in cognitive psychology before deciding to pursue graduate studies in computer science. I see technology as a great enabler to helping improve both access to care and quality of care.

Outside of my professional life I keep myself amused with a variety of hobbies, such as writing music, rock climbing, and snowboarding.
